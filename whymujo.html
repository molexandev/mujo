ENERGY AND STRESS CONNECTION

Your cells sense stress through a mechanism called the mitochondria, which also produces molecules that store your energy (ATP, adenosine triphosphate). Exposure to stress can switch energy production off, making you feel tired and unproductive.

Stress comes in many forms

Environmental toxins

Psychological

Poor diet

Poor health /Illness

Poor gut health

Chronic inflammation
